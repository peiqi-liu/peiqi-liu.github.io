Hello, I am Peiqi!

I am currently a robotics engineer intern at Hello Robot under the supervision of Dr. Chris Paxton and a research assistant at NYU Courant under the supervision of Prof. Lerrel Pinto.

I am currently focused on robot semantic memory, open vocabulary navigation, vision launguage models. Besides this, I am also interested in long horizon robotics and mobile manipulation in general. My short term goal is to transfer powerful zero shot vision and multi-modal models into open vocabulary visual grounding systems that can be used in robot navigation. My long term goal is to build generalist robots working life long.
